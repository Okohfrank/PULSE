Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need the internet to use PULSE? ⌄
The core triage engine runs entirely offline — it is embedded in the app and requires zero internet. When you open PULSE on your clinic phone, the Service Worker caches the entire app. Generator runtime calculations, triage protocols, and appliance load shedding all work without signal. The AI Co-Pilot and cloud sync require an internet connection, but the life-saving triage never does.
Does PULSE require any hardware installation? ⌄
No. PULSE is designed to work with what your clinic already has — a generator, a phone, and nothing else. The app runs on any Android phone. Manual data input (fuel level, NEPA status) is enough to get full triage functionality. Smart switches are an optional Phase 2 upgrade that allow PULSE to automatically cut non-critical loads — but they are not required to start.
What happens after my 14-day Pro trial ends? ⌄
You are automatically moved to the Free plan. Nothing is deleted. Your clinic profile, appliance registry, and crisis log history are all preserved. You can upgrade back to Pro at any time. We will not charge you without explicit confirmation.
How does the AI Co-Pilot work in local languages? ⌄
The language selector in the Co-Pilot screen (Pro feature) sends your choice to Claude's system prompt. Claude is then instructed to respond entirely in Yoruba, Hausa, or Igbo — regardless of what language you type your question in. It understands questions in any language and replies in whichever language you selected. The language setting persists across your session.
Can the government or an NGO pay for our clinic? ⌄
Yes — this is exactly how Enterprise works. A state health ministry, WHO, UNICEF, Gates Foundation, or any NGO can take an Enterprise licence and provide Pro access to hundreds of clinics under a single contract. Individual clinics receive full Pro features without managing their own subscription. Contact us at hello@chemxai.com to discuss bulk licensing.
Is our clinic's data private and secure? ⌄
All data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) with Realtime Database security rules that ensure each clinic can only access their own data. We do not sell, share, or advertise against your clinic's energy data. As the ChemXAI platform owner, we have administrative access to operate and maintain the system, but your patient data never enters PULSE — only energy metrics.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept bank transfer, Paystack (cards, USSD, bank transfers), and Flutterwave. For Enterprise clients, we accept purchase orders and invoicing on Net-30 terms. We do not currently accept cash payments.
